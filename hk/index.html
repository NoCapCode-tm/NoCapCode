FAQs

We're here to help

FAQs designed to provide the information you need.

What kind of companies do you work with?

We work with early-stage founders, small teams, and growing businesses that need reliable software systems not just quick prototypes. Most of our work involves MVP development, internal tools, automation, and scalable product systems.

Can you help if I’m not sure what needs to be built yet?

Yes. Many projects start with clarity, not code. We help define scope, remove unnecessary features, and make decisions before development begins often saving time and cost later.

Do you only build MVPs, or do you also improve existing systems?

We do both. We build new products from scratch and also improve existing software that has become fragile, slow, or hard to change. The goal is always stability and long-term usability.

How do you price and scope projects?

We scope work based on clarity, not guesses. After understanding your problem, we define what should be built, what shouldn’t, and what risks exist. Pricing follows that reality not fixed packages.

Are you an agency or a long-term partner?

We're neither a typical agency nor a staffing service. We act as an execution partner focused on decision quality, system stability, and outcomes whether that’s a short engagement or ongoing work.
